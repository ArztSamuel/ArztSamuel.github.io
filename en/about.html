Who is Samuel Arzt

My interest in computer sciences also lead me to two internships at the University of Klagenfurt, where I was able to gather a lot of experience in areas like teamwork or orginisation.

After graduating from grammar school I served my civilian service at the Austrian Red Cross for 9 months.
