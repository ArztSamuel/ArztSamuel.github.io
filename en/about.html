Who is Samuel Arzt

I am 21 years old, born in Austria, Carinthia, and at the time I am living in Salzburg where I am studying MMT at the University of Applied Sciences FH-Salzburg. During my studies I was asked to hold the tutorials for the lectures "Applied Mathematics 1", "Introduction to Programming" and "Introduction to Computergraphics".

In 2014 I graduated from grammar school BRG-Viktring. Since I was very interested in computer sciences, I chose it as my compulsory optional subject and also decided to major in mathematics and computer sciences for my school leaving examination. A lot of my Java Projects actually originate from this time.

My interest in computer sciences also lead me to two internships at the University of Klagenfurt. During my first internship I worked in a team of 3 on programming a small Lego-NXT robot using C#. We programmed the robot to be able to be controlled with hand gestures using Microsoft's Kinect. My second internship was about programming an AR Parrot drone to recognise a specific pattern with its onboard camera and to follow said pattern. In a team of 4 people we programmed a simple user interface in Java which also visualised the analytical processes of the drone. During my internships I could gather a lot of experience, particularly in areas like teamwork and organisation.

After graduating from grammar school I served my civilian service at the Austrian Red Cross for 9 months.

After my first year at university, I was able to participate in an internship at Redox - Game Labs, where I worked as a C# - Developer in a Unity-Project in a team of 7 people. After my internship Redox decided to hire me as a part time employee, alongside my studies.
