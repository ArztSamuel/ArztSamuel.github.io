3D Rubik's Cube

A small simulation of a functioning Rubik's Cube in 3D.

Beschreibung

I used OpenTK (a C# wrapper for OpenGL) to program this simulation of a Rubik's Cube. I implemented simple phong-lighting with GLSL, as well as mouse-picking and simple shadow-mapping.

The Cube can be moved with the mouse. Likewise the individual sides of the Cube can be rotated.

Time of Development:

Mid 2016
