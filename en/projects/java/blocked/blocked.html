Blocked

Description:

One of my Java-based games. It is not even close to being finished, but a first prototype is done. It is a 2D Top-Down Zombie-Shooter.

The character is controlled with AWSD and the mouse.

Like for most of my projects I also have not used any libraries or engines for this game. The whole game all the way down to the "Game-Loop" has been developed by me. Even the graphics were hastily drawn by myself. I have also programmed a map-creator for this game.

Time of Development:

Late 2014 - Mid 2015 (to be continued...)
